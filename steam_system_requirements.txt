Viking Settlement Tycoon — Steam System Specifications

Windows
Minimum:
- OS: Windows 10 (64-bit)
- Processor: Intel Core i3-6100 or AMD FX-6300
- Memory: 4 GB RAM
- Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 750 Ti, AMD Radeon R7 360, or Intel UHD 620 (integrated) with 1 GB VRAM
- DirectX: Version 11
- Storage: 500 MB available space
- Additional Notes: 16:9 and 16:10 aspect ratios recommended. Runs on integrated graphics at lower settings.

Recommended:
- OS: Windows 10/11 (64-bit)
- Processor: Intel Core i5-8400 or AMD Ryzen 5 2600
- Memory: 8 GB RAM
- Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 1050 Ti / GTX 1650 or AMD Radeon RX 570 with 4 GB VRAM
- DirectX: Version 12
- Storage: 500 MB available space
- Additional Notes: 1080p with smooth frame rates and enhanced visuals.

macOS
Minimum:
- OS: macOS 11 Big Sur (Intel or Apple Silicon via Rosetta 2)
- Processor: Intel Core i5 (2015+) or Apple M1
- Memory: 4 GB RAM
- Graphics: Intel Iris Plus / Apple M1 integrated
- Storage: 500 MB available space
- Additional Notes: Use Safari/Chromium-based runtime for best compatibility.

Recommended:
- OS: macOS 12 Monterey or newer
- Processor: Apple M1/M2 or Intel Core i7 (2018+)
- Memory: 8 GB RAM
- Graphics: Apple M1/M2 integrated or AMD Radeon Pro (4 GB)
- Storage: 500 MB available space
- Additional Notes: 1080p or higher with stable 60 FPS.

Linux
Minimum:
- OS: Ubuntu 20.04 LTS or equivalent (64-bit)
- Processor: Intel Core i3-6100 or AMD FX-6300
- Memory: 4 GB RAM
- Graphics: OpenGL 3.3 compatible GPU (Mesa drivers supported), 1 GB VRAM
- Storage: 500 MB available space
- Additional Notes: Tested on recent Mesa/NVIDIA proprietary drivers.

Recommended:
- OS: Ubuntu 22.04 LTS or equivalent (64-bit)
- Processor: Intel Core i5-8400 or AMD Ryzen 5 2600
- Memory: 8 GB RAM
- Graphics: OpenGL 4.1 / Vulkan-capable GPU with 4 GB VRAM
- Storage: 500 MB available space
- Additional Notes: Chrome/Chromium runtime recommended.

Steam Deck (Playable)
- Default: Playable at 1280×800 on Medium settings
- Recommended: 40–60 FPS with battery saver mode
- Notes: Controller/trackpad supported. On-screen keyboard may be used for naming.

General Notes
- Input: Mouse/Keyboard required on PC; Touch supported on mobile builds.
- Network: Offline play supported; cloud save optional.
- Save Files: Small footprint; export/import available as Base64 TXT.
- Audio: Standard stereo output; no dedicated audio hardware required.
