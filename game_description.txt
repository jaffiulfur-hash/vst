Viking Settlement Tycoon — Game Overview

Lead your clan from a humble camp to a thriving Norse settlement. Explore an infinite, procedurally generated world of fjords, forests, tundra, and mountains; gather resources; construct iconic buildings; and grow your people into a legendary community worthy of the sagas.

Core Features
- Infinite World: Seamlessly generated biomes (coastal fjords, boreal forests, arctic tundra, temperate plains, highlands) with unique terrain and visuals.
- Settlement Building: Place and upgrade Longhouses, Farms, Lumber Mills, Blacksmiths, Trading Posts, and Temples—each affecting resources and stats.
- Resource Economy: Manage food, wood, iron, and gold with clear per-second production feedback.
- Scouts & Exploration: Dispatch scouts to unveil the map, pierce the fog of war, and find strategic build locations.
- Day/Night Cycle & Weather: Dynamic lighting, sun rays, and atmospheric events like distant thunder and lightning.
- Clean, Minimal UI: Functional layout with clear stats (Happiness, Defense, Prosperity) and intuitive build panel.
- Sound & Ambience: Satisfying UI clicks, build effects, resource chimes, ambient forests and oceans, and a triumphant Viking horn.

Platforms
- PC/Desktop: Full experience with mouse/keyboard support and optional Steam Cloud sync (Electron + steamworks.js when available).
- Mobile (Android): Optimized touch controls, pinch-to-zoom, double tap to zoom, long press to send scouts, and a portrait splash screen.

Performance
- Frame Pacing: 30 FPS VSync lock for stable performance across a wide range of hardware.
- Smart Loading: Chunks stream in/out around the camera for smooth exploration.
- Lightweight Assets: Efficient rendering and caching for sprites and terrain.

Controls (PC)
- WASD: Move camera
- Mouse Drag: Pan
- Mouse Wheel: Zoom
- Right-Click: Send scout to target
- E: Export save to TXT (Base64-encoded)
- I: Import save from TXT
- N: New territory (reset seed, fresh start)
- P: Photo mode (screenshot)
- ESC: Cancel building placement

Saving & Cloud
- Local Saves: Automatic and manual saves stored in localStorage.
- Export/Import: Base64 UTF-8 TXT export for backups and sharing.
- Steam Cloud: If running under Electron with steamworks.js, cloud sync is supported (safe no-op in browser).

Buildings (Examples)
- Longhouse: +Population growth; core housing for your clan.
- Farm: Sustains food production for long-term growth.
- Lumber Mill: Produces wood to unlock higher-tier structures.
- Blacksmith: Increases iron production and boosts Defense.
- Trading Post: Generates gold and enhances Prosperity.
- Temple: Improves Happiness and cultural prestige.

Goal
Balance growth, resources, and wellbeing while exploring and mastering harsh northern lands. Let your settlement prosper through smart planning and bold exploration—your saga awaits.
